About me

This blog serves mostly as a reference guide for me; when I learn something new, I usually write it down in case I forget it. Sometimes some of that knowledge might end up here :)

I work as a QA at Cognifide. My professional interests revolve mostly around software development and data analysis. These days most of my learning time is devoted to NLP (everything in the still-existing gap between how computer science and linguistics look at human languages) and computer vision (especially text recognition).

Others things I enjoy: linguistics, astrophysics, RPGs, well-made food, and dank memes.

I also co-organize Rails Girls Poznan and will gladly support any other diversity-oriented non-profits.

If you have an interesting book to recommend, the best way to contact me is via email.
